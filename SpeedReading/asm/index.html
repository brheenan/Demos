Performance of asm.js optimizations

This test drive demo compares the performance of eight asm.js benchmarks from JetStream, Massive, & Octane with and without asm.js optimizations. The benchmarks ran are bigfib.cpp, box2d, container.cpp, float-mm.c, gcc-loops.cpp, & zlib.

The larger the bar, the better the performance. In browsers with asm.js optimizations, including support for the “use asm” directive, the “with asm” bar will be larger while in browsers without optimizations the bars will be equal lengths.
